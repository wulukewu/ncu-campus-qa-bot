	1.	學位考試細則第三條：如有特殊情形，經系所主管同意後，得採視訊方式辦理；惟仍應符合公平公正公開原則，並全程錄音錄影存檔留存於系所，經系所主管同意方得調閱。相關檔案須妥予保存一年，惟依規定提起申訴者，應保存至申訴程序結束或行政救濟程序結束為止。
Article 3 of National Central University Rules Governing the Master’s and Doctoral Degree Qualifying Exam and Thesis Defense stipulates that the thesis defense can be conducted via video conferencing only under certain circumstances upon approval of the chair of the department or institute. The thesis defense should also be conducted in a fair, impartial, and transparent manner. The defense should be recorded during the whole process, and the recording of the defense can be accessed only with the permission of the chair. Relevant files should be properly stored for one year. When anyone who follows relevant regulations to file an appeal regarding the defense, those relevant files should be kept until the appeal procedure or procedure for administrative remedies is completed.
