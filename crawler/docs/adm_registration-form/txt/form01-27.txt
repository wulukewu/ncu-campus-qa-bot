《國立中央大學學則》第  40  條  
學生每次得申請休學一學期至二學年。休學累計以二學年為原則，期滿因重病或特殊事故等原因需再申請休學者，經系（學位學程）主管及教務長同意，得酌予延長休學二年。
Article 40 of National Central University Study Regulations
Students may apply for suspension of schooling for one semester to two academic years each time. The maximum of the cumulated period of suspension of schooling is two academic years. For the applicant who needs to apply for a longer period of suspension of schooling due to any serious illness or incident, an additional period of two more academic years can be allowed upon approval from the head of the applicant’s department (degree program) and the vice president for academic affairs.
